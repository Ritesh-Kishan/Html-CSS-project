More About Us



Getting your first job in a High-Tech is not a joke. Not only it requires extremely deep and solid knowledge of programming languages and innovative technologies, but also a unique way of thinking you can only learn from an experienced mentor.
EduLabs' mission is not just to teach you a course, but to turn you into a lucrative well-paid High-Tech professional that constantly gets job suggestions from Facebook and Google.
How do we achieve this goal?


  • All our lecturers are proven experts in their field with years of experience in High-Tech industry, and currently work in High-Tech. We don't accept lecturers that don't have practical knowledge of the subjects they are teaching (you'll be amazed how many mentors have never practiced what they teach)
  • We build our course syllabuses and constantly update them based on demands in High-Tech companies and innovations in the industry. (Be prepared: this industry is changing rapidly!)
  • We prepare you for your job interviews and make everything for you to succeed.
  • We make you a part of the Israeli High-Tech community from day one since a good network and connections to people in the industry can drastically increase your chances of getting a job.
